Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What is the Minimum Order Quantity? ▼
MOQ is 20 pieces per order. You can mix & match any designs or colors. All future reorders must also be in multiples of 20 pieces.
Do you offer credit or COD? ▼
No. We only accept prepaid orders via Razorpay. This helps us maintain rock-bottom pricing and fast shipping for everyone.
How fast is shipping? ▼
We pack & dispatch within 1-2 days. Delivery across India takes 3-5 business days. Free shipping on orders ₹5,000+. Below that, flat ₹150 shipping charge.
What's the difference between Standard and Premium? ▼
Standard designs (₹60/pc) are original characters like Flexi Bunny, Tiger, Shark, etc. Premium designs (₹75/pc) feature licensed characters like Pikachu & Eevee. Both have 100% profit margins.
Do you offer volume discounts? ▼
Yes! Order 100+ total pieces in a single order and get 10% discount automatically applied. Works on any mix of Standard and Premium designs.
Can I request custom colors? ▼
Custom colors are not available for standard B2B orders. However, we regularly add new colors to existing designs. Check back weekly for updates!
How often do new designs launch? ▼
Every week! We add 1-2 new designs to keep your inventory fresh. Subscribe to updates or check back every Friday for new launches.
How does the referral program work? ▼
Share your unique referral code with other retailers. When they order ₹5,000+, you earn ₹1,200 store credit instantly. Use it for future orders. No limit to how many people you can refer!
What's your return/exchange policy? ▼
We guarantee product quality. If you receive damaged items, contact us within 48 hours with photos. We'll ship replacements immediately. Manufacturing defects covered at no cost.
